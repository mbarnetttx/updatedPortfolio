Get to know me!


If you asked my peers about me, they would probably say that I am positive, resourceful, and determined. I was a young mother and was so proud when I finished college. I wanted to be a teacher. However, the cruel brutality of children was too much for my peaceful way of life. If you have someone in your life that is a teacher, give them a hug, they need it!

I dipped my toes in a few different professions after that, trying to find career paths that interested me. In the meantime, I practiced graphic design, which I studied in college as well. Most importantly, I helped to build a business and website called 512ATX, which is a "search by category" site containing almost every restaurant and bar in Austin, Texas.

I also worked on a site called Down To Fest, which sold and promoted clothing and accessories made by vendors in the music festival community. For the past 6 months, I have been working on my own site, DiscoThreads, a retail store featuring my own graphics. It has been my baby ever since.

When I heard about the UT Bootcamp, I knew immediately that it would help to expand my personal endeavors. I would love to be a part of a successful company, but I would like to create a company and help others achieve success as well.
